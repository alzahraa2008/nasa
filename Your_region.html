California

CO2 Emissions: High due to transportation and industrial sectors, but decreasing with renewable energy adoption.

Climate Conditions: Warm Mediterranean climate, experiencing increased wildfires and droughts.

Mitigation: Aggressive goals to reduce emissions with solar power and electric vehicles.

CO2 Emissions: One of the highest in the U.S. due to oil and gas production.

Climate Conditions:Variable, with extreme heat, hurricanes, and droughts.

Mitigation:Transitioning to wind power, leading the nation in wind energy
